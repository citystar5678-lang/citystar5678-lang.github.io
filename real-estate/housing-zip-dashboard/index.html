Explore Columbus Ohio housing trends by ZIP code. Type a ZIP to focus on one area — or select multiple ZIPs to compare.

How to Use

Open the ZIP filter in the dashboard, type your ZIP (e.g., 43212), and select it.

Select more than one ZIP in the same filter to view side-by-side trends.

Median sale price, inventory, months of supply, and more.

Columbus Ohio — all ZIP codes (Redfin market tracker data).

Months of Supply (MOS) estimates how long it would take to sell all current listings at the current pace of sales. Seller’s Market — MOS < 3 months Low inventory → strong demand. Sellers usually have more leverage; prices tend to rise or stay firm. Balanced Market — ~3 to 6 months Supply and demand are roughly even. Prices are more stable. Buyer’s Market — > 6 months Higher inventory → buyers have more choices. Sellers may need to negotiate more, and prices can soften.
